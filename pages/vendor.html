Seller Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

This Seller Policy outlines the terms and conditions for vendors and sellers who wish to sell their products on the Groots Technoedge platform. By registering as a seller, you agree to comply with all policies, guidelines, and terms outlined in this document.

2. Seller Registration and Onboarding

2.1 Eligibility Requirements

To become a seller on our platform, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Have a valid business license (if applicable)
  • Provide accurate business information and documentation
  • Have a valid bank account for payments
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Agree to our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy

2.2 Registration Process

The seller registration process includes:

  1. Completing the online seller application form
  2. Submitting required business documents (GST certificate, business license, etc.)
  3. Verification of business information and documents
  4. Account approval and activation
  5. Completing seller profile setup

2.3 Account Verification

We reserve the right to verify all seller information and may request additional documentation at any time. Failure to provide accurate information may result in account suspension or termination.

3. Product Listing Guidelines

3.1 Product Requirements

All products listed on our platform must:

  • Be authentic and accurately described
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Meet our quality standards
  • Not infringe on intellectual property rights
  • Not be prohibited or restricted items
  • Have clear, high-quality product images
  • Include accurate product descriptions and specifications

3.2 Prohibited Products

The following products are strictly prohibited:

  • Counterfeit or replica items
  • Illegal or restricted products
  • Products that violate intellectual property rights
  • Hazardous or dangerous materials
  • Products that violate our community guidelines
  • Items that are offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate

3.3 Product Images

Product images must:

  • Be clear, high-resolution, and professionally taken
  • Show the actual product (not stock photos unless authorized)
  • Include multiple angles and close-up details
  • Not contain watermarks, logos, or promotional text
  • Comply with our image quality standards

4. Pricing and Commission

4.1 Pricing Guidelines

Sellers are responsible for setting competitive and fair prices for their products. We reserve the right to:

  • Review and approve product pricing
  • Suggest pricing adjustments
  • Reject listings with unreasonably high prices
  • Implement promotional pricing during sales events

4.2 Commission Structure

Our commission structure is as follows:

  • Standard Commission: 15% of the product sale price
  • Premium Sellers: 12% commission (for sellers meeting specific criteria)
  • Sale Events: Additional 5% commission during promotional periods

Commission is calculated on the final sale price (excluding taxes and shipping charges).

4.3 Payment Terms

Seller payments are processed:

  • On a bi-weekly basis (every 14 days)
  • After order fulfillment and delivery confirmation
  • Minus applicable commissions and fees
  • Via bank transfer to the registered account

5. Order Fulfillment

5.1 Order Processing

Sellers must:

  • Process orders within 24-48 hours of receiving them
  • Update order status promptly
  • Ship products within the specified timeframe
  • Provide accurate tracking information
  • Ensure products are properly packaged

5.2 Shipping Requirements

Sellers are responsible for:

  • Arranging product shipping to customers
  • Using reliable shipping partners
  • Providing tracking numbers
  • Ensuring products arrive in good condition
  • Meeting delivery timelines as promised

5.3 Inventory Management

Sellers must:

  • Maintain accurate inventory levels
  • Update product availability in real-time
  • Remove listings for out-of-stock items
  • Notify us immediately of any inventory issues

6. Quality Standards

6.1 Product Quality

All products must meet our quality standards:

  • Products must be new and unused (unless listed as pre-owned)
  • Products must match their descriptions accurately
  • Products must be free from defects and damage
  • Products must comply with safety and quality regulations

6.2 Quality Inspections

We may conduct random quality inspections of products. Sellers found selling substandard or misrepresented products may face:

  • Product listing removal
  • Account warnings
  • Temporary or permanent account suspension
  • Legal action if applicable

7. Customer Service

7.1 Seller Responsibilities

Sellers are responsible for:

  • Responding to customer inquiries within 24 hours
  • Resolving customer complaints promptly
  • Handling returns and refunds according to our policy
  • Maintaining professional communication with customers
  • Providing accurate product information

7.2 Customer Ratings and Reviews

Sellers must maintain:

  • Minimum 4.0-star average rating
  • Response rate of at least 90%
  • On-time delivery rate of at least 95%
  • Low cancellation rate (less than 5%)

Failure to meet these standards may result in account restrictions.

8. Returns and Refunds

Sellers must comply with our Return and Refund Policy:

  • Accept returns within 7 days of delivery
  • Process refunds within 5-7 business days
  • Handle defective or damaged products appropriately
  • Cover return shipping costs for defective items
  • Cooperate with our customer service team

9. Prohibited Practices

Sellers are prohibited from:

  • Manipulating product reviews or ratings
  • Engaging in fraudulent activities
  • Circumventing platform fees or commissions
  • Contacting customers outside the platform for transactions
  • Listing products on competing platforms at lower prices
  • Using misleading or false product information
  • Violating intellectual property rights
  • Spamming or harassing customers

10. Account Suspension and Termination

10.1 Grounds for Suspension

We may suspend or terminate seller accounts for:

  • Violation of this Seller Policy
  • Poor performance metrics
  • Customer complaints and disputes
  • Fraudulent or illegal activities
  • Non-compliance with quality standards
  • Repeated policy violations

10.2 Appeal Process

If your account is suspended, you may appeal by:

  1. Contacting our seller support team
  2. Providing relevant documentation
  3. Explaining the circumstances
  4. Proposing corrective actions

11. Intellectual Property

Sellers must:

  • Own or have rights to use all product images and descriptions
  • Not infringe on third-party intellectual property
  • Obtain necessary licenses and permissions
  • Respect our brand guidelines when using our platform

12. Data Protection

Sellers must comply with data protection laws and:

  • Protect customer information
  • Use customer data only for order fulfillment
  • Not share customer data with third parties without consent
  • Comply with our Privacy Policy
