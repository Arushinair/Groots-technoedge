Refund, Return & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Return Policy

At Groots Technoedge, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 7 days of delivery.

1.1 Return Eligibility

Items are eligible for return if they meet the following conditions:

  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original condition
  • Original tags and labels must be attached
  • Items must be in their original packaging
  • Proof of purchase (invoice) must be provided
  • Items must not be damaged, altered, or customized

1.2 Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items without original tags or labels
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or used
  • Items damaged due to misuse or negligence
  • Items purchased during sale or promotional events (unless specified otherwise)
  • Underwear, innerwear, and personal hygiene products

1.3 Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@groots.co or call +91 9784777188
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Our team will provide you with a Return Authorization Number (RAN)
  4. Package the item securely with the original packaging
  5. Ship the item to the address provided by our customer service team

2. Refund Policy

2.1 Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

2.2 Refund Timeline

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item
  • UPI: 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item
  • Bank Transfer: 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item

2.3 Shipping Charges

Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective product, etc.). In such cases, we will refund the full amount including shipping charges.

3. Exchange Policy

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item:

  1. Return the original item following our return process
  2. Place a new order for the desired item
  3. Once your return is processed, you will receive a refund for the original purchase

4. Cancellation Policy

4.1 Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it, provided the order has not been shipped. To cancel:

  • Contact us at orders@groots.co or call +91 9784777188
  • Provide your order number
  • We will process the cancellation and issue a full refund

4.2 Cancellation by Groots

We reserve the right to cancel any order due to:

  • Unavailability of the product
  • Pricing errors
  • Suspected fraudulent activity
  • Technical issues

In such cases, you will receive a full refund within 5-7 business days.

For any queries regarding returns, refunds, or cancellations, please contact us:
