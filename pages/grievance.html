Grievance Redressal Policy

(as per Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021)

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

In compliance with the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021, Groots Technoedge Pvt. Ltd. has established this Grievance Redressal Policy to address user complaints and grievances in a timely and effective manner.

2. Grievance Officer

Name: Grievance Officer

Designation: Customer Relations Manager

Email: grievance@groots.co

Phone: +91 9784777188

Address: Groots Technoedge Pvt. Ltd., India

Response Time: Within 24 hours of receiving the complaint

3. Types of Grievances

We address the following types of grievances:

  • Complaints regarding content on our website
  • Issues related to product quality or description
  • Order and delivery-related complaints
  • Payment and refund issues
  • Privacy and data protection concerns
  • Violation of terms of service
  • Any other user-related grievances

4. How to File a Grievance

4.1 Online Submission

You can file a grievance by:

  1. Sending an email to grievance@groots.co
  2. Including the following information:
    • Your full name and contact details
    • Order number (if applicable)
    • Detailed description of the grievance
    • Supporting documents or screenshots (if any)
    • Date and time of the incident

4.2 Phone Submission

You can also call our grievance helpline at +91 9784777188 during business hours (Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM IST).

4.3 Written Submission

You may send a written complaint to our registered office address. Please ensure to include all relevant details and contact information.

5. Grievance Resolution Process

5.1 Acknowledgment

Upon receipt of your grievance, we will:

  • Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
  • Provide a unique grievance reference number
  • Inform you of the expected resolution timeline

5.2 Investigation

Our grievance officer will:

  • Review your complaint thoroughly
  • Investigate the matter with relevant departments
  • Gather necessary information and documentation
  • Contact you if additional information is required

5.3 Resolution Timeline

  • Simple grievances: Resolved within 3-5 business days
  • Complex grievances: Resolved within 15-30 business days
  • Legal or regulatory matters: As per applicable laws and regulations

5.4 Resolution Communication

Once your grievance is resolved, we will:

  • Communicate the resolution via email or phone
  • Provide details of any actions taken
  • Request your feedback on the resolution

6. Escalation Process

If you are not satisfied with the resolution provided by our Grievance Officer, you may:

  1. Request escalation to senior management by emailing escalation@groots.co
  2. File a complaint with relevant consumer forums or regulatory authorities
  3. Seek legal recourse as per applicable laws

7. Content Removal Requests

If you believe any content on our website violates your rights or applicable laws, you can request removal by:

  1. Sending a detailed email to grievance@groots.co
  2. Including:
    • URL of the content in question
    • Reason for removal request
    • Supporting documentation
    • Your contact information
  3. We will review and respond within 24 hours
  4. If the content violates our policies or applicable laws, we will remove it within 36 hours

8. Confidentiality

All grievances are handled with strict confidentiality. We will not disclose your personal information or complaint details to any third party except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

9. Record Keeping

We maintain records of all grievances received and their resolutions for a period of at least 2 years, as required by applicable laws.
