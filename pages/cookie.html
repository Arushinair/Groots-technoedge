Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies allow a website to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions. This helps us provide you with a better experience when you browse our website.

2. How We Use Cookies

Groots Technoedge uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember items in your shopping cart
  • Analyze how you use our website
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Deliver relevant advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt-out of these cookies.

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
  • Authentication Cookies: Used to keep you logged in to your account
  • Security Cookies: Help protect against fraud and ensure website security

3.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website's performance and user experience.

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Time spent on pages
  • Error messages and loading times
  • Traffic sources and demographics

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They remember your choices and preferences to provide a more personalized experience.

  • Language preferences
  • Region or location settings
  • Product preferences and wishlist
  • Shopping cart contents

3.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests. They also help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

  • Track your browsing behavior across websites
  • Build a profile of your interests
  • Show you relevant advertisements
  • Limit the number of times you see an ad

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance tracking
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing
  • Social Media Platforms: For social sharing and integration
  • Advertising Networks: For delivering relevant advertisements

These third parties may use cookies to collect information about your online activities across different websites. We do not control these cookies, and you should review the privacy policies of these third parties.

5. Cookie Duration

5.1 Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are essential for the website to function during your visit.

5.2 Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience on future visits.

  • Short-term: Expire within 24 hours
  • Medium-term: Expire within 30 days
  • Long-term: Expire after 1 year or more

6. Managing Cookies

6.1 Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Note: Blocking or deleting cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

6.2 Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner when you first visit our website. You can change these preferences at any time by:

  • Clicking on the cookie settings link in the footer
  • Accessing your account settings
  • Clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our website

6.3 Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of certain third-party cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Advertising: Visit Your Online Choices to opt out of interest-based advertising

7. Mobile Devices

If you access our website on a mobile device, you may need to manage cookies through your device settings. The process varies depending on your device and browser:

  • iOS (Safari): Settings > Safari > Block Cookies
  • Android (Chrome): Chrome menu > Settings > Site settings > Cookies

8. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly:

  • You may need to re-enter information repeatedly
  • Your shopping cart may not save items
  • You may not be able to access certain areas of the website
  • Personalized features may not work
  • Website performance may be affected

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
