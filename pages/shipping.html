Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Shipping Information

Groots Technoedge ships to all major cities and towns across India. We use reliable shipping partners to ensure your orders reach you safely and on time.

1.1 Shipping Partners

We partner with leading logistics companies including:

  • Blue Dart
  • Delhivery
  • India Post
  • DTDC

2. Delivery Timelines

Delivery times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected:

Location Standard Delivery Express Delivery
Metro Cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune) 3-5 business days 1-2 business days
Tier 2 Cities 5-7 business days 2-3 business days
Other Cities & Towns 7-10 business days 3-5 business days

Note: Delivery timelines are calculated from the date of dispatch, not the date of order placement. Orders are typically dispatched within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.

3. Shipping Charges

3.1 Standard Shipping

  • Orders above ₹999: Free shipping
  • Orders below ₹999: ₹99 shipping charges

3.2 Express Shipping

  • Additional charges of ₹199 apply for express delivery
  • Available only in select metro cities
  • Orders must be placed before 2:00 PM IST for same-day dispatch

4. Order Processing

4.1 Processing Time

Orders are typically processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days. During sale periods or high-demand seasons, processing may take 3-5 business days.

4.2 Order Confirmation

Once your order is placed, you will receive:

  1. Order confirmation email with order details
  2. Payment confirmation email (if payment is successful)
  3. Shipping confirmation email with tracking number once the order is dispatched

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order status:

  • Through the tracking link provided in your shipping confirmation email
  • By logging into your account on our website
  • By contacting our customer service team

6. Delivery Attempts

Our delivery partners will make up to 3 delivery attempts. If all attempts fail:

  • The order will be returned to our warehouse
  • You will be contacted to reschedule delivery
  • Additional shipping charges may apply for re-delivery

7. Delivery Issues

7.1 Delayed Delivery

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, please contact us immediately. We will investigate and keep you updated on the status.

7.2 Damaged or Lost Packages

In case of damaged or lost packages:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery (or expected delivery date)
  • Provide photos if the package is damaged
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund

8. International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping will be available soon. Please check back for updates.
